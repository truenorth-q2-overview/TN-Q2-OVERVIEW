Educational Methodology

True North's pillars of education provide a holistic learning experience that develops both the character and intellect of our students through three interrelated components.

✨

Awaken Your True North

Development of character qualities through conscious leadership. We nurture students' abilities to interact and adapt to their dynamic surroundings, emphasizing both individual and collective well-being, ensuring students mature into compassionate leaders.

🏗️

Project-Based Learning

Our curriculum integrates competencies with real-world applications, challenging students to address tangible global issues. Each quarter culminates in a project that harmonizes academic learning with character development, fostering initiative, persistence, and critical thinking.

📚

Foundational Literacies

We emphasize essential proficiencies in reading, writing, mathematics, scientific understanding, cultural, civic, and emerging literacies like technology and finance. These are integrated into everyday scenarios so students apply knowledge in practical, meaningful contexts.
